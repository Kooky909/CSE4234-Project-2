About me

Tommy Galletta is a current student at the Florida Institute of Technology majoring in Computer Science, and is currently on track to graduated December 2024. He is currently working under Dr. Debasis Mitra in the Biomedicial Computing Lab on campus. In the lab he has helped to create simulations for PET and SPECT imaging, as well as working with neural networks to perform basic reconstructions based on simulation data.

Tommy grew up in Wappingers Falls, New York, and currently lives in Knoxville, Tennessee when he is not on campus. When he isn't studying, Tommy likes to sing, read, and play Dungeons & Dragons. In the future he hopes provide entertainment to the world in some way or another, whether it be by using his computer skills to create a video game, or combining those skills with others to create other forms of entertainment, such as performances.

Tommy believe that if you are passionate about something and put your mind to it, you can achieve whatever your goals are. He always tries to put out the best work he can, learn from his mistakes, and improve constantly to create the best product possible.
